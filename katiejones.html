Listen: Six Months in Saint Paul - A Freshman in Congress

Host Raj Maitra sits down for a candid conversation with Peter Schmitt, the husband of first-term State Representative Katie Jones. Together, they pull back the curtain on what it’s actually like when a family member transitions from a community advocate to a legislator in the Minnesota House of Representatives.

Representative Katie Jones

Katie Jones represents District 61A in the Minnesota House of Representatives, which includes Central Downtown, Elliot Park, Loring Park, the Wedge, Harrison, Bryn Mawr, East Isles, Lowry Hill, Kenwood, and Cedar-Isles-Dean. She assumed office on January 14, 2025, bringing her unique background as an engineer and climate policy expert to the legislature.

Originally from Lebanon, Indiana, Jones earned her Bachelor of Science in Biological and Agricultural Engineering from Purdue University. Before her election, she worked as a community program and policy manager at the Center for Energy and Environment, where she collaborated on landmark climate and transportation legislation. She served as Chair of the Lowry Hill East Neighborhood Association and was a member of the Minneapolis Capital Long-Range Improvement Committee, demonstrating her commitment to local governance and community engagement.
